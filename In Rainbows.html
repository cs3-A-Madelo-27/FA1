In Rainbows

  • In Rainbows is the seventh studio album by Radiohead.
  • It was self-released on 10 October 2007.
  • Followed by a physical release internationally through XL Recordings on 3 December 2007.
  • It was Radiohead's first release after their recording contract with EMI ended with their album Hail to the Thief (2003).
